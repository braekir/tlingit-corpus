1       Tle wê / wùch kìká áwé át has datîyin yú.á,
2       yâdu á wé łkùdasêts'k,
3       yâdu á wé Was'as'ê.
4       Á áwé tle wùch tugêt has xhʼawdli.át.
5       Tle kʼadên wùch ìn has datîyin,
6       chʼa wâ sáyá has at kàwa.àkhw tle chʼa wùch xhʼayáxh.
7       Á khu.as giant
8       khudzitìyi át yê has ayasâkw.
9       Á áwé
10	tle chʼa yâkʼudé awé chʼu tle khuwdudlihà.
